Exercise Sets 46–48

Cover Weeks 11–12 material: triple integrals and change of variables (§6.5–6.6), cylindrical and spherical coordinates (§6.7), and the Divergence Theorem (§6.8).

★ Study Strategy

Green's, Stokes', and the Divergence Theorem (Sets 43–48) are the capstone of the course. Practice recognizing WHICH theorem applies: Green's for 2D closed curves, Stokes' for open surfaces with boundary curves, Divergence for closed surfaces enclosing volumes. Cylindrical/spherical coordinate conversion (Set 47) is tested heavily on the final.
