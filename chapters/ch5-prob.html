Recommended Problems

Suggested homework from the course outline. Do as many as you can and check answers in the solutions manual.

Exercise Sets 37–38

Cover Week 7 material (second half): double integrals and iterated integrals (§5.1–5.2). Practice setting up integration bounds for rectangular and non-rectangular regions.

Exercise Sets 39–40

Cover Week 8 material: applications to volume under a surface (§5.3) and change of variables in double integrals — polar coordinates and Jacobians (§5.4).

Exercise Sets 41–42

Cover Week 9 material (first half): sketching surfaces in 3D space — quadric surfaces, cylinders (§5.5), and parametric equations of surfaces (§5.6).

★ Study Strategy

Changing the order of integration (Sets 37–38) and converting to polar coordinates (Sets 39–40) are essential skills that appear on every test covering this material. Sketching 3D surfaces (Sets 41–42) builds intuition for surface integrals in Ch 6.
