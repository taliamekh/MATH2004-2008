💡 Insider Tips

From the W26 cohort:

  • Several long answer questions were reused from the 2019 final with different numbers — practice those solutions above
  • At least two MCQ were copy-pasted from Lucy's in-term tests — review your midterms
  • When div F is a constant, don't bother integrating — just multiply by the volume of the region (cylinder = \(\pi r^2 h\))
  • For Stokes' on a plane: the unit normal comes straight from the plane equation \(ax+by+cz=d \implies \mathbf{n}=\frac{(a,b,c)}{|(a,b,c)|}\). Skip parameterizing the boundary curve entirely
  • Only ~1 triple integral question appeared — don't over-study triple integral mechanics
  • YouTube resources recommended by students: Houston Math Prep Calc 3 and Professor Leonard
